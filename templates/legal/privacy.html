Privacy Policy

Yalla Habibi is designed using privacy-aware architectural principles intended to minimize unnecessary data exposure and support responsible AI interaction.

Privacy-First Design Philosophy

  • Minimal data dependency
  • Context-limited processing
  • No intentional personal data commercialization
  • User-centric interaction model

Regional Privacy Considerations

The platform is developed with awareness of modern global privacy expectations, including frameworks commonly associated with:

  • European data protection principles (GDPR-inspired practices)
  • California consumer privacy concepts (CCPA / CPRA-aligned thinking)
  • General international data minimization standards
  • Privacy-respectful interaction norms

While Yalla Habibi is not presented as a legally certified compliance system, its design philosophy emphasizes responsible handling of user interactions and signals.

User Signal & Privacy Mechanisms

Yalla Habibi follows interaction patterns that reduce persistent identity tracking and favor session-scoped behavior.
