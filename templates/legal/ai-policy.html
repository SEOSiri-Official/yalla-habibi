AI Transparency & Responsible Use

Yalla Habibi is a multilingual AI-powered voice assistant designed to support cross-language communication in a responsible and transparent manner.

How the AI Works

Yalla Habibi uses advanced language processing and speech recognition technologies to generate responses. The system processes user input and returns AI-generated replies.

Limitations

  • Responses may be incomplete, outdated, or incorrect.
  • The system is not a substitute for legal, medical, or financial advice.
  • Outputs should be independently verified when accuracy is critical.

Data Handling

Voice and text inputs may be processed through secure third-party AI services to generate responses. Yalla Habibi does not intentionally store personal data unless explicitly required for system functionality.

Responsible Usage

Users must not use the system for harmful, abusive, illegal, or deceptive purposes. Violation of these guidelines may result in restricted access.

Continuous Improvement

The AI system is continuously improved to enhance safety, reliability, and user experience.
